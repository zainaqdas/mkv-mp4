How it works
MKVs with H.264 + AAC/MP3 play instantly — browser handles them natively with zero processing.
Other codecs (HEVC, VP9, AC3, DTS, TrueHD) are transcoded by FFmpeg running inside your browser.
No files are uploaded anywhere. Everything runs locally using WebAssembly.
Large files (1GB+) may be slow to transcode — browser RAM is the limit.
Powered by FFmpeg.wasm — runs entirely in your browser
No uploads · No servers · No tracking
